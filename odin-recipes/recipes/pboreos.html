PB Oreo Sandwich

Description

This was meant to be a PB Oreo sandwich but we ran out of Oreos. It's still mouthwatering right?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter (ONLY PEANUTS, unsalted)
  • Salt
  • Oreos, but we didn't have them

Steps

  1. Spread PB on the bread
  2. Sprinkle salt on top
  3. Close the sandwich
  4. Enjoy!
